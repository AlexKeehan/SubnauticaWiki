Eggs

Eggs within Subnautica are the unhatched variants of their respective Fauna counterparts. Almost all eggs can be used to hatch their respective creatures. Eggs can only be found in the respective creatures biomes.

A common egg the player will encounter is the Ampeel Egg, which can be found in Blood Kelp Zone and Bulb Zone. Once found, this egg can be placed in an Alien Containment structure and will hatch after several in-game days.
