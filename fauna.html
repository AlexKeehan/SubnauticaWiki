Fauna

On Planet 4546B, fauna can be found everywhere within the Crater's Edge. Fauna can come in all shape, size, and forms. From a tiny rock dwelling Rockgrub to the massive Sea Dragon Leviathan. Fauna is often extremely adapted to their environments, which results in incredibly diverse and unique species. Fauna can range from passive herbivores to extremely aggressive apex predators. Caution is always advised when approaching a species on this planet.
