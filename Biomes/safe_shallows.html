Safe Shallows

The Safe Shallows is the starter biome for new players and contains sprawling coral reefs and small cave systems. As the name implies, this shallow biome is one of the safest in the game, with no predatory creatures. However, there are environmental hazards and it is quite easy to be disoriented by the winding caves and run out of oxygen. Some predators can wander into this biome from the Kelp Forest and Grassy Plateaus.

This biome offers plentiful diving experiences for new players, with a variety of flora, fauna, and resources. At night time, this biome truly shows its alien nature with plentiful bioluminescence.
