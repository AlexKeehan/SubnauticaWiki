Kelp Forest Caves

The Kelp Forest Caves are a cave system located at deeper depths within the Kelp Forest. These caves offer dangerous conditions and are difficult to traverse due to their darkness, hazardous flora, and their winding nature. Valuable resources can be found in these caves, but with a concentration in the deeper areas.
