Lost River

This alien biome is a massive cave biome found connecting some of the deepest biomes ever recorded on Planet 4546B. Entrances to this biome can be found in multiple biomes, including Blood Kelp Zone (Northern), Deep Grand Reef, Bulb Zone, and Blood Kelp Zone (Trench). This biome is a vast gloomy biome, with a greenish tint from its alkaline brine pools that can be found throughout. Exploration is highly dangerous due to the number of hostile creatures that call this biome home, including a Ghost Leviathan (Juvenile). Ancient skeletons of leviathans long dead can be found here, with the biggest being the 3 million-year-old Gargantuan Leviathan skeleton that, if alive, would measure over a kilometer long, which is more than half of the entire Crater's Edge.
