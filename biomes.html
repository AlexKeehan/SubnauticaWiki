Biomes

In Subnautica, biomes are distinctive regions that host varied ecosystems brimming with unique flora, fauna, and resources. These biomes contribute to the game's extensive navigation and exploration elements, that provide the player with numerous settings to explore and discover. Biomes range from lush Kelp Forests to harsh Lava Lakes that offer a broad range of challenges and rewards.

The first biome the player will experience is the Safe Shallows , which offers a respite from the inhospitable ocean. As the player progresses, they will encounter more treacherous areas such as the Dunes and Lost River , which contain hazardous features, hostile creatures, and hidden leviathans. Biomes vary in atmosphere and visuals, with each offering the player a unique view on the vibrant planet of 4546B.
